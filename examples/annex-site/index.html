Annex a Store

An annex script will allow you to take over some aspects of another storefront, replacing it with zoovy functionality. For instance, you may not want to rebuild your entire storefront, but to use the zoovy API for cart and checkout. To do so, you would use one of the following scripts:

  • Magento Store (add to cart, cart and checkout)
  • Yahoo Store (add to cart, cart, checkout and write reviews)

These differ from other apps in that the templates are in independant files. This is to minimize the footprint of the annex script.
