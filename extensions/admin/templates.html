          Fashion

          Fashion is intended for apparel and jewelry sellers, however it could be easily adopted for any industry.

          The main navigation bar, homepage banner and homepage product lists rely heavily on product tagging, specifically new arrivals, best sellers, sale and clearance items.

          The category pages feature subcategory and product imagery at a 1x1 aspect ratio (square).

          Mobile

          Mobile is intended for all sellers, for use on their mobile domain. The site is responsive, so it will work on a variety of screen resolutions and devices.

          The homepage product lists rely heavily on product tagging, specifically new arrivals and best sellers. The various pages (product and category) are designed for a smaller screen size.

          The category pages feature subcategory and product imagery at a 1x1 aspect ratio (square).

          B2B

          B2B is indended for business to business sellers. It's a no-frills website that offers the buyer the ability to purchase directly from the category page and in bulk.

          The category pages feature subcategory and product imagery at a 1x1 aspect ratio (square).
