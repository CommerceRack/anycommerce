                                      All orders of $50 and more qualify for free standard shipping. Applicable only to address in the continental United States (HI and AK are not included).                                       All preorders paid with a credit card will be charged at the time of shipment. All preorders paid with Google Checkout, Paypal or Amazon Payments will be charged when your order is placed. Orders that have both preorder and instock items we be shipped at the same time in one shipment. We recommend that you do not order in stock items with preorder items.
