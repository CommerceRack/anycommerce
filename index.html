                                            All orders of $50 and more qualify for free standard shipping. Applicable only to address in the continental United States (HI and AK are not included).                                             All preorders paid with a credit card will be charged at the time of shipment. All preorders paid with Google Checkout, Paypal or Amazon Payments will be charged when your order is placed.
